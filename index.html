About us

Transcorn comprises of a team of Young Mentors with great abilities who are making a difference in the world. They do transformational mentorship to different classes of people to be able to bring out all rounded personalities

With absolute skills in IT, the main aim is to create a platform where young ladies in Africa can learn basic IT skills and be able to join in the IT field which is currently male dominated. We do not limit ourselves to ladies , We are all rounded

We also Nature talents and encourage people to explore and utilize most opportunities that they have.
